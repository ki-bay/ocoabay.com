Vineyard & Wine

OcoaBay produces high-quality tropical wines in Azua, Dominican Republic, with vineyards yielding two harvests per year. The resort cultivates a variety of European and American grape strains, including the notable French Colombard variety. OcoaBay's wine cellar and tasting room offer an exceptional wine-tasting experience, showcasing their wines' unique flavors and quality. The resort aims to compete globally, leveraging the Caribbean climate and soil to produce distinctive, top-tier wines for wine enthusiasts and connoisseurs.

Gastronomy

OcoaBay's cuisine offers a delectable fusion of traditional Dominican flavors and contemporary culinary techniques. Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients are crafted into exquisite dishes that reflect the vibrant, tropical essence of the region. Each meal is a gourmet experience, designed to complement their fine wines and provide an unforgettable dining experience.
